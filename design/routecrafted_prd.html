# RouteCrafted: Product Requirements Document ## Vision & Positioning RouteCrafted is a travel planning assistant that turns scattered research into a structured, adjustable plan. **Core Promise:** "Keep the plan practical as weather, time, and traveler preferences change." ## Target Users * **Primary:** Leisure travelers (25–45), mobile-first, looking for fast planning for city breaks or short holidays. * **Secondary:** Couples, friend groups, and families needing simple day plans and quick attraction summaries. ## Key Business Functionalities 1. **Itinerary Creation:** Destination, dates, budget, style, group type, and pacing. Generates day-by-day plans with activities, meals, and backups. 2. **Weather-Aware Replanning:** Monitors forecasts and suggests revisions for rain, heat, etc. 3. **Worth-it / Skip-it Cards:** Short mobile cards for every attraction with summaries, pros/cons, cost, and time. 4. **Save, Edit, and Regenerate:** Versioning and full CRUD for trips. 5. **Account & Trip Management:** Profiles, favorites, and trip history. 6. **Admin Operations:** Content quality management, prompt templates, and user support. ## Design Goals * **Practicality:** Focus on feasibility (nearby grouping, duration estimates). * **Decision Support:** Reduce fatigue with scannable cards. * **Flexibility:** Easy to swap or rewrite parts of the plan.
